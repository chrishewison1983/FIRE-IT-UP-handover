What is FIRE IT UP!?

The 'Fire It Up' campaign launched on 17th January 2019 to promote apprenticeships to potential apprentices and employers. This included national TV and social media advertising, which can be found on the dedicated Fire It Up YouTube channel. The Fire It Up campaign website is designed to support this campaign, inspiring and informing potential apprentices, employers and parents.
